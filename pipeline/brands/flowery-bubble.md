# Flowery Bubble 泡沫花市

## Brand Summary
Flowery Bubble is a Chinese womenswear label specializing in romantic knitwear and feminine outerwear. The brand is known for soft, voluminous silhouettes with intricate handcrafted details—three-dimensional embroidery, delicate lace insertions, and playful patchwork. Their pieces feel like wearable poetry, blending nostalgic femininity with contemporary craft.

## Aesthetic & Style
- **Core aesthetic:** Romantic maximalism meets cozy elegance
- **Signature details:** 3D floral embroidery, lace trim, bubble/voluminous silhouettes, patchwork
- **Color palette:** Soft neutrals (cream, grey, brown), pastels, occasional bold accents
- **Mood:** Dreamy, poetic, cottagecore-adjacent but refined
- **References:** Think Cecilie Bahnsen's sculptural femininity meets Ganni's playful knitwear

## Materials & Quality
- Premium natural fibers: alpaca, mohair blends, high-grade wool
- Hand-finished embroidery and lace details
- Quality construction justifying mid-premium pricing
- Soft, tactile fabrics designed for comfort and longevity

## Price Positioning
- **Wholesale/Taobao:** ¥200-400 (~$30-60 USD)
- **International retail:** $100-180 USD
- **Comparable to:** Ganni, & Other Stories, Sézane, ba&sh
- **Value proposition:** Designer-level details and materials at accessible luxury prices

## Target Customer
- Women 20-35
- Style-conscious, Instagram-aware
- Values unique pieces over fast fashion
- Drawn to feminine, romantic aesthetics
- Willing to invest in standout knitwear and statement outerwear
- Appreciates craft and quality materials

## Product Categories
1. **Knitwear** (core strength): Cardigans, sweaters, knit dresses
2. **Outerwear:** Patchwork jackets, leather accent pieces, quilted coats
3. **Tops:** Lace-detailed blouses, embroidered shirts

## Naming Convention
Products often have poetic Chinese names translated to evocative English:
- "冬日恋歌" → "Winter Love Song"
- "梦幻岛" → "Dream Island"
- Names should feel romantic, slightly whimsical

## Brand Voice for Copy
- **Tone:** Warm, evocative, sensory
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon, generic descriptors, fast-fashion language
- **Emphasize:** Tactile qualities, craftsmanship, the feeling of wearing the piece
- **Sample phrases:**
  - "Wrapped in cloud-soft alpaca..."
  - "Delicate embroidery blooms across..."
  - "A cardigan that feels like a gentle embrace..."

## Why Axent Customers Should Care
Flowery Bubble represents the best of emerging Chinese design: meticulous craftsmanship, premium materials, and a distinct aesthetic voice—at a fraction of comparable European designer prices. Each piece is a discovery, offering the quality and uniqueness typically reserved for brands three times the price.
